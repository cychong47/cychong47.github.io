  • Multi-layer architecture - pioneers the use of Ethernet fronthaul and any combination of distributed and centralized deployments
  • Processing capacity is allocated from almost anywhere in the network, such as an adjacent cell or a centralized data center, to where it is needed most for coordination and capacity.

  • Ready for the upcoming demands of 5G core and radio demands
